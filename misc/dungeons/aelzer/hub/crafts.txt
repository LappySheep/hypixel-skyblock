At the right Aelzer level, you are able to craft various items. This is another method of obtaining special items other than drops and buying them from Nil'Eix.



Materials
  - Crying Obsidian [RARE]
    Requires Aelzer Level 8.
    Recipe: 128 Enchanted Obsidian + 64 Enchanted Lapis Lazuli



Weapons
  - Stalker Blade [EPIC]
    Requires Aelzer Level 8.
    Recipe: Stalker Hilt + 512 Enchanted Eyes of Ender
      +280 Damage
      +110 Strength
      +0.1% Life Steal
      Item Ability - Blight Stalker RIGHT CLICK
        Gain +20% bonus damage for the next 2 seconds. Killing a monster refreshes the duration of Blight Stalker and increases the bonus damage by 5%. Stacks up to +100%.
        Cooldown: 180s
        Cooldown starts after Blight Stalker expires.

  - Null Staff [EPIC]
    Requires Aelzer Level 4.
    Recipe: Vuoi'h's Null Stalk + 48 Crying Obsidian
      +260 Damage
      +220 Intelligence
      Item Passive - Intelligence Haste
        Passively, casting Null Strike or a magic basic attack on an enemy increases your base Intelligence by 2% (additively), up to a maximum of 20%. Lasts for 5 seconds. Decays while out of combat for at least 6 seconds.
      Item Ability - Null Strike RIGHT CLICK
        Shoot a blast of null energy in the target direction, dealing [4,500 + 33x Intelligence] magic damage to the first target hit, and slowing them by 20% for 2 seconds. Killing a target with Null Strike causes it to bounce to nearby other enemies for 10% less damage per bounce, up to a maximum of 5 enemies total.
        Mana Cost: 480
        Cooldown: 16s

  - Pane of Pain [LEGENDARY]
    Requires Aelzer Level 5.
    Recipe: 6 Ancient Glass
    +235 Damage
    +190 Strength
    Item Passive - Salt in the Wound
      Targets marked from Shardsplitter take 66% bonus damage as a 3-second bleed. Duration stacks, damage does not.
    Item Ability - Shardsplitter RIGHT CLICK
      Shoot a fragment of the pane in the target direction. The first enemy hit takes 120% Weapon Damage. The main target and up to 3 nearby enemies get marked for 4 seconds. Mark duration refreshes when attacked.
      Cooldown: 90s
        Cooldown is reduced by 6 seconds on each hit.

    Full Set Bonus - Abyssal Aura
      Every player within 5 blocks of each other with a full Abyssal set gain 25% bonus crit damage each, stacking up to 125% extra.
