My soul is ready.

For humanity, our race will finally ascend as what was foretold; an extension of the Fallen One himself.

This dungeon houses the sacred beings, those that forged our universe, yet wishes to crash down upon our lives as if we were fodder... useless. As if we were their toys.

Yet today, from this point onwards, we will stand above all odds. The Gods that once lay waste to our world will fall beneath us.
