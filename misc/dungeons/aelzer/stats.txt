Pure Defence
  Reduces incoming Pure Damage.
    Formula:
    r = x/[x+100]

Depth Resistance
  Required to do well in Aelzerian Rift floors. If you do not have enough Depth Resistance, you will only have a portion of your usual stats.
  Base: 1 Depth Resistance
    Formula:
      m = x/r (if x < r) else 1

Lethality

    Formula:
    r = x/[x+100]
