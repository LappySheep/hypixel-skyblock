8 Bottles of Corrosion [RARE]
  Forge Recipe: 4 Acidic Solutions + Refined Mithril
    Optional:
      - Add (up to 6) Refined Tantalum to increase the amount of bottles forged per slot by 4 each.
  Forging Time: 2h
  Throw the bottle at a location to create a corroded region (2 block radius). For the next 4 seconds, Mining Speed is increased by 5% against corroded blocks.
  Upgrades (BoCs can stack in brewing stands):
    Radius: 2 blocks -> 2.5/4/6.5 blocks (Gunpowder / Enchanted Gunpowder / Enchanted Firework)
    Duration: 4s -> 7/20/80s (Redstone Dust / Enchanted Redstone / Enchanted Redstone Block)
    Mining Speed Increase: 5% -> 20/50/90% (Glowstone Dust / Enchanted Glowstone / Enchanted Glowstone Block)
