Sentinel Squid:
    45,000 HP
    300 Defence
    "Aquatic Tethering" - The Sentinel tethers itself to the player that deals the most damage to it, dealing 600 damage per second and reducing healing by 60%.
    "Whirlpool Dash" - Occasionally, the Sentinel will dash through other players, knocking them vertically up to 35 blocks and dealing 2800 damage to all targets hit.
    "Oceanic Implosion" - If the Sentinel knocks a player up and they land in the water nearby, the Sentinel may create a small implosion that pulls other players in and deals 4500 damage or 15% of the target's maximum health, whichever is higher.

Tearing Eye [Eye of Ender]:
    [EPIC] A tear dropped by a Sentinel Squid.
    Recipes:
        > 8 Tearing Eyes + 64 Enchanted Ink Sacs --> 1 Inky Eye
        > 4 Inky Eyes + [Any Weapon or Bow] --> Inked [Tier #] [Weapon Name]
            Inked weapons/bows apply a 25% slow on-hit for 3 seconds. You can use up to 40 Inky Eyes to upgrade a weapon to tier 10. Each tier increases the total damage multiplier by 10% (double damage at Inked 10).
        > 4 Inky Eyes + 4 Enchanted Lily Pads + Saving Grace --> Shadow's Grace
            Shadow's Grace is consumed if you are about to die, creating an explosion (no kb) that deals true damage to nearby enemies equal to your maximum health, and granting 25 Strength and 25 Defence with a full health restoration.
Sentinel Squids also drop between 2 and 6 Enchanted Ink Sacs.

Rod of Ages [LEGENDARY]:
    +100 Damage
    +100 Strength
    +20% Crit Damage
    Every 10,000 Fishing EXP earned increases the damage of the Rod of Ages by 1, capped at 400 (500 total bonus). Every Sea Emperor and Sea Empress slain with the Rod of Ages getting the last hit will increase the strength bonus by 1, capped at 125 (225 bonus).
