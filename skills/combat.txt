Right click a sword while shifting to go into Fury for 3 seconds, causing consecutive melee hits to deal 1% more damage, up to 6%.
Right click an axe while shifting to go Berserk for 3 seconds, causing melee hits to deal bonus flat true damage equal to 6% of your total strength.
Left click a bow while shifting to go into a Rampage for 3 seconds, causing each of your bow shots to slow targets within 6 blocks by 20% for 2 seconds, and gain 10% lifesteal on bow shots.
