Foraging Milestone Pet - Ent

Find higher rarities of the Ent pet by breaking more logs!

Common: 5,000 Logs
Uncommon: 25,000 Logs
Rare: 125,000 Logs
Epic: 500,000 Logs
Legendary; 2,000,000 Logs



Common Ent [Lv100]
    Speed: +40

    Ent Splitter
        Breaking a log has a 10% chance of breaking up to 1 additional nearby block.



Uncommon Ent [Lv100]
    Speed: +40

    Ent Splitter
        Breaking a log has a 20% chance of breaking up to 1 additional nearby block.



Rare Ent [Lv100]
    Speed: +40

    Ent Splitter
        Breaking a log has a 20% chance of breaking up to 1 additional nearby block.

    Forest Aura
        Cutting trees causes logs within 6 blocks become protected by your Aura; reducing the effect of Jungle Axes and Treecapitators from other players by 33%.



Epic Ent [Lv100]
    Speed: +40

    Ent Splitter
        Breaking a log has a 25% chance of breaking up to 1 additional nearby block.

    Rejuvenation
        Gain +45 Speed for 3 seconds when a tree respawns within 15 blocks.



Legendary Ent [Lv100]
    Speed: +40

    Ent Splitter
        Breaking a log has a 25% chance of breaking up to 1 additional nearby block.

    Forest Aura
        Cutting trees causes logs within 6 blocks become protected by your Aura; reducing the effect of Jungle Axes and Treecapitators from other players by 50%.

    Speaking for the Trees
        Cutting at least half of a tree reduces the time required for it to respawn by 50%.
