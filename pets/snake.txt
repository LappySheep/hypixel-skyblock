Legendary Snake [Lvl 100]
Combat Pet

Strength: +30
Speed: +40

Strafing Slither
    Strafing left/right while sprinting builds up 15 speed per second, up to a maximum bonus of 150. Bonus lasts for 8 seconds after stopping.

Venom Fang
    Taking damage from a monster or attacking one aggros the Snake onto them; attacking once every two seconds for 60x your Strength as damage over 2 seconds.

Miasma
    Attacking a target affected by Venomous will double the slow and deal 45% bonus damage as DoT over 1.5 seconds.
