Legendary Goose [Lvl 100]
Combat Pet

Strength: +60
Crit Damage: +60%

Honk Honk
    Every fifth melee attack in a row within 3 seconds, honk loudly to cleave nearby monsters (and players at level 100) for 20% bonus damage and knockback.

Firmly Grasping Bill
    Increase your item pickup range of standard items by 2 blocks and of special items by 1 block. Items collected have a 30% chance of counting towards collection twice.

Ferocity
    Monsters that stay within 3 blocks for longer than a second take 20% bonus damage, up to 60% bonus damage after 3 seconds. Ferocity resets after 8 attacks.
