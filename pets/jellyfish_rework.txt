[Lvl 100] Jellyfish
Alchemy Pet

Speed: +30
Intelligence: +100
Magic Find: +5

Radiant Regeneration
  Increase your base HP regen by 100%. Heal allies within 7 blocks for 20% of the total HP you regenerate. Radiant Regeneration is tripled in Dungeons.

Iridescent Rays
  Damaging an enemy within 4 blocks grants +5 Speed to every ally within 5 blocks for 3 seconds. Stacks up to a maximum of 50 Speed.

Blinding Ring
  While in Dungeons, using an ultimate ability blinds monsters within 10 blocks, causing melee attacks to miss, for 5 seconds. Additionally, grant 50 bonus Strength to allies within 5 blocks for 3 seconds after using your Normal Class Ability.
